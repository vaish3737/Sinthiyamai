That is how Sinthiyamai was initially formed.The word is coined from the world's first language "Tamil".My Sinthiyami is kind of double entendre,it as two meanings."Mai" refers to ink and "Sinthiya" refers to spilled so together it means Spilled ink.The second meaning of Sinthiyamai can be perceived from the same word which when syllabified by other means "Sinthi" and "Amai" means "to think" and "silence" thinking in silence.So consolidated I would like to put my tagline as "Thoughts spilled in silence!"
